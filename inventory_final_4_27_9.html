  2. Click Extensions → Apps Script. Delete everything, paste below.

⚠ Reset Data

Choose what to clear. This cannot be undone.
📦
Reset Stock Quantities Only
Sets all item quantities (Left + Bought) to zero. Keeps your items, categories, prices, and costs — just wipes the amounts.
🗒️
Clear Activity Log Only
Deletes all sales history, orders, and activity entries. Keeps your inventory items, stock levels, prices, and settings untouched.
🧹
Wipe All Logs — Keep Inventory
Clears ALL activity logs, EOD logs, staff buy logs, cancelled orders, customer credits, VIP status, customer deals, and day notes. Your inventory items (names, prices, stock, flavors) are untouched.
🗑️
Remove All Items
Deletes every item from every category. Categories themselves are kept. Logs are kept. Costs and settings are kept.
💥
Full Reset — Wipe Everything
Clears all inventory, all logs, all credits, all costs. Keeps your login names, passphrases, categories, and commission settings.
